How it works

Three steps, start to finish.

A clear path to adding value to your property, with an Australian based support person at the other end of every application.

01

Quick eligibility check

A few questions about you and your property: how much you'd like to borrow, what the project is, and whether you're comfortable with the loan being secured against your property. You'll get an instant indication, with no impact on your credit file.

Check eligibility →
02

Full application

If it's a fit, complete the rest online: your details, your property and any existing mortgage, the amount and purpose, and a snapshot of your income and expenses. It takes a few minutes, and you can add a partner or referral code if you have one.

Start an application →
03

We assess & come back to you

Our team reviews your application against our credit policy and verifies your details. We'll come back to you quickly with the outcome and next steps. If approved, we settle the loan and the funds are made available for your project.

What you'll need handy

Your property details

The address of the property the loan will be secured against, and details of any existing mortgage.

Income & expenses

A simple picture of your income and regular outgoings so we can assess affordability.

Identification

An Australian driver licence or passport. We verify your identity securely during assessment.
