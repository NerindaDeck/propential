Target Market Determination

The class of customers this product is designed for, and how it is to be distributed.

In short

  • Designed for owners of Australian property seeking $5,000–$100,000 of secured, fixed-term finance for a property project, who are comfortable with security over their property and able to meet principal & interest repayments.
  • Not designed for people who do not own property, or who want unsecured or revolving (line-of-credit) finance.
  • Distribution is through Propential and its approved partners. This determination is reviewed periodically and when review triggers occur.
This is a working draft.
