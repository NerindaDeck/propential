Hardship

If you’re having trouble meeting your repayments, we’re here to help.

In short

  • If you’re experiencing financial difficulty, contact us as early as you can. You can request a hardship arrangement and we’ll work with you on the available options.
  • We assess each request on its circumstances and will let you know the outcome and any next steps.
  • Free, independent financial counselling is available through the National Debt Helpline on 1800 007 007.
